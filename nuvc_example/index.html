What is NUVC?

Every successful entrepreneur has an awesome pitch, and here’s ours: we’re offering university students the chance to become part of the fastest-growing, most innovative entrepreneurship competition in the country. Application Process

Teams interested in competing in NUVC will need to submit an executive summary outlining their startup proposal. Competitors will be judged in the preliminary round based on the quality of the business model, sustainability, market opportunities, and finances. Teams advancing to the semi-finals and finals round will need to prepare a five and seven minute presentation respectively. Each presentation will be followed by a five minute question & answer period during which the judges may ask the teams specific questions regarding the startup plan.

Applications will open in January 2015.

F.A.Q.

Who can compete?

This competition is open to anyone with at least one Northwestern undergraduate or graduate student with a primary role within the startup. However, for the first time, NUVC is allowing Northwestern alumni to sponsor anyone - any student, any university - as a Wildcard entry, making NUVC 2015 the most open challenge yet.

Can I still enter the competition if I have already received outside funding?

Yes, but you must report all monetary amounts you have earned. This includes any additional funding you may have gotten after you have submitted your application.

Can I be a part of more than one team?

Yes, you can be a part of more than one team.
